## Tips for Effective Outreach

1. **Personalise every email** — mention a specific article, page, or detail about the recipient. Never copy-paste blindly.
2. **Follow up once** — wait 5–7 days, send a brief polite nudge. Don't follow up a third time.
3. **Track everything** — update `seo/link-prospects.csv` with dates and statuses.
4. **NAP consistency is non-negotiable** — use the exact address, phone, and business name everywhere.
5. **Singapore tone matters** — polite but not overly formal. Use "Cheers" / "Warm regards" not "Sincerely". Reference PSLE/O-Level/A-Level naturally.
