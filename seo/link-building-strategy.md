## Notes

- **NAP consistency is critical**: Use the exact NAP block (Section: Tier 1) on every single listing.
- **Do NOT buy links or use link farms** — Google penalises this. All opportunities here are genuine editorial/directory.
- **KiasuParents uses nofollow** but drives massive referral traffic and brand trust — arguably more valuable than SEO alone.
- **Serangoon CC and Serangoon Public Library** are both within walking distance of Blk 265 — highest geographic advantage.
- **Ace Scorers** is the best competitor model — similar size, replicable strategy.
- All URLs should be verified before submission; some may have changed.
- Track all submissions in `seo/link-prospects.csv`.
